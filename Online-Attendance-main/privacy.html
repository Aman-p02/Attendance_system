Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Welcome to SmartAttend. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect, use, and store the following types of personal data about you:

  • Identity Data: Full Name, Roll/Enrollment Number, Division, and Semester.
  • Contact Data: If you reach out to us via email for support, we may store your email address.
  • Usage Data: Attendance records, timestamps, and login sessions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data for the core functionality of the SmartAttend platform:

  • To register you as a new student or teacher.
  • To manage and track class attendance accurately.
  • To prevent proxy attendance using short-lived OTP sessions.
  • To generate attendance reports for faculty members.

3. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered, or disclosed. We use secure cloud databases (Supabase/PostgreSQL) with encrypted passwords.

4. Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfil the educational and administrative purposes we collected it for.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact the developer at amanjp5711@gmail.com.
