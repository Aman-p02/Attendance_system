Add SmartAttend to Your Home Screen

Install SmartAttend like an app no Play Store needed!

"Add to Home Screen" option missing?
  • WhatsApp / Instagram Users: You cannot install the app if you opened it by clicking a link in chat. Click the 3 dots and select "Open in Chrome" first.
  • Redmi / Xiaomi Users: Your phone blocks Chrome from making shortcuts by default. Go to Phone Settings → Apps → Chrome → Permissions and allow "Home Screen Shortcuts".
STEP 1
Open in Chrome

Open this website in Chrome or any Chromium-based browser on your Android phone.

STEP 2
Tap Menu (⋮)

Tap the three-dot menu button at the top-right corner of your browser.

STEP 3
Add to Home Screen

Select "Add to Home screen" from the dropdown menu that appears.

STEP 4
Confirm & Done!

Confirm the name "SmartAttend" and tap Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

How to Signup & Login

Follow these simple steps to get started with SmartAttend.

STEP 1
Choose Role

Click on "Login Now" and choose whether you are a Student or a Teacher.

STEP 2
Go to Sign Up

If you are a new user, click on the "Sign Up" link at the bottom of the login page.

STEP 3
Register Details

Fill in your details properly (like Roll No, Division, Semester) and create your account.

STEP 4
Login & Use

Return to the login page, enter your new credentials, and start marking attendance!
